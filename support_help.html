Frequently Asked Questions

Q1: How do I cast my vote?
A1: To cast your vote, select your candidate, confirm your ID, and click the 'Submit Vote' button.
Q2: What if I forgot my ID?
A2: You must have a valid ID to vote. Please retrieve it before proceeding.
Q3: How do I check my voting history?
A3: Go to the 'Voting History' section in your account dashboard to view all past votes.
Q4: Can I change my vote after submission?
A4: No, once a vote is cast, it cannot be changed to ensure the integrity of the process.
